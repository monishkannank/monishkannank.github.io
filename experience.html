Research Analyst — Corporate Actions

FactEntry Data Solutions (a SIX Financial Information company) · Fixed Income Financial Services · Vellore, TN

EFFECTIVE AUG 2025
STATUS:ACTIVE
  • Captures and processes 800–1,000 corporate action events/month into the DCA database at a 99%+ accuracy rate (2–5 discrepancies per 500 entries), within SLA.
  • Performs reconciliation and QC across ex-date, record date, pay date, rates, currencies and ISIN data; runs database audits for discrepancies, duplicates and missing fields.
  • Processes mandatory and voluntary categories — redemptions, interest & coupon events, reorganisations, distressed debt, and equity structural events (mergers, acquisitions, spin-offs) — across multiple currencies and geographies.
  • Reviews issuer documentation, prospectuses, regulatory filings, exchange announcements and corporate communications to validate events against source.

  • Since June 2026: moved from production capture onto ISO-aligned processing — DTCC REDEMG (MCAL/PCAL from CRPB) and DTCC RAW (ISO 20022, voluntary events) files, plus Euroclear settlement files (ISO 15022, voluntary events) — resolving file-level rejections and their root causes.
  • Supports DCA system UAT with field-level test documentation and edge-case scenarios; contributes to SOP documentation for team consistency and onboarding.
  • Trained and onboarded 4 analysts on DCA workflows, quality benchmarks and event classification; go-to resource for corporate actions queries across roughly half the team.
